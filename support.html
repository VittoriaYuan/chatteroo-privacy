Frequently Asked Questions

Account Related

Q: How do I sign in?
A: Click the "Sign in with Google" button and use your Google account to sign in.
Q: Can I change my account?
A: Yes. In the Profile Menu > Manage Account, you can sign out and sign in with a different account.
Q: How do I delete my account?
A: Go to Profile Menu > Manage Account > Delete Account. After deletion, all your data will be permanently deleted.

Messages Related

Q: How many messages can I send per day?
A:
  • New users: 3 messages per day
  • After submitting feedback: 6 messages per day
Q: When does the message quota reset?
A: The message quota resets automatically at midnight (00:00) based on your device's timezone.
Q: How do I check my message quota?
A: In the Profile page, tap on "Messages Remaining Today" to view detailed information.
Q: How long are messages saved?
A: Messages are saved until you delete your account.

Features Related

Q: How do I submit feedback?
A: In the Profile Menu > Message Quota Details page, tap the "Share Feedback" button.
Q: How long does it take to unlock extra messages after submitting feedback?
A: The extra messages are available immediately after submitting feedback. You can use 6 messages per day starting from the same day.
Q: Can I turn off the background music?
A: In the current version, background music plays automatically. We may add an option to disable it in future versions.

Technical Issues

Q: What should I do if the app crashes?
A:
  1. Try restarting the app
  2. If the problem persists, please contact us through the feedback feature
  3. Make sure your device is running iOS 16.6 or later
Q: I can't send messages. What should I do?
A:
  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Make sure you have remaining message quota
  3. Try signing out and signing in again
Q: I'm not receiving push notifications. What should I do?
A:
  1. Check your device's notification settings
  2. Make sure the app has notification permissions
  3. Check if notifications are disabled in the app settings
