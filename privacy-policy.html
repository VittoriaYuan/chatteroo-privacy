Chatteroo Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 26, 2024

Introduction

Chatteroo ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

By using Chatteroo, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information (Required for Authentication)

When you sign in using Google OAuth, we collect the following information from your Google account:

  • Email Address: Your Google email address (required for account creation and authentication)
  • Name: Your full name (if provided by Google)
  • Profile Picture URL: Your Google profile picture URL (if available)
  • Google User ID: A unique identifier provided by Google

Why we collect this:

  • Email address is required for account authentication and to provide you with access to the App
  • Name and profile picture are used to personalize your experience within the App
  • This information is necessary for the App to function properly

Note on Anonymity: While we require a Google email address for authentication purposes, your messages and conversations with the forest animals are private and not shared with other users. Your email is used solely for account management and is not displayed to other users.

1.2 User-Generated Content

  • Messages: The messages you send to the forest animals
  • Tags/Emotions: Any emotion tags you select when composing messages
  • Feedback: Any feedback you submit about the App

Why we collect this:

  • Messages are necessary to generate AI-powered replies from forest animals
  • Tags help us provide better context-aware responses
  • Feedback helps us improve the App

1.3 Usage Data

  • Message Quota: Daily message count and quota usage
  • Timezone: Your device timezone (for daily quota reset)
  • App Usage: General usage patterns to improve the App experience

1.4 Device Information

  • Device Identifier: For push notifications (if enabled)
  • Platform Information: iOS version and device type (for technical support)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 To Provide Our Services

  • Process and store your messages
  • Generate AI-powered replies from forest animals
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Send push notifications when you receive replies
  • Reset daily message quotas based on your timezone

2.2 To Improve Our Services

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the App
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features
  • Respond to your feedback

2.3 For Legal Compliance

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Protect our rights and the rights of our users
  • Respond to legal requests

3. Information Storage and Security

3.1 Storage Location

Your information is stored securely in:

  • Supabase Cloud Database: All user data, messages, and profiles are stored in Supabase's secure cloud infrastructure
  • Server Location: Data is stored on Supabase servers, which may be located outside your country of residence

3.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted Transmission: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Secure Authentication: Google OAuth provides secure authentication
  • Access Control: Row Level Security (RLS) policies ensure only authorized access
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored in secure databases with access controls

Important: While we take reasonable security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

3.3 Data Retention

  • Account Information: Retained until you delete your account
  • Messages: Retained until you delete your account or individual messages
  • Feedback: May be retained in anonymized form for App improvement purposes
  • Usage Data: Retained for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

4.2 We Do NOT Share with Advertisers

We do not share your information with advertisers or advertising networks.

4.3 Service Providers

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

Google OAuth

  • Purpose: User authentication
  • Data Shared: Email, name, profile picture (as provided by Google)
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Supabase

  • Purpose: Data storage, authentication, and backend services
  • Data Shared: All user data, messages, and account information
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

POE API

  • Purpose: AI-powered reply generation
  • Data Shared: Your messages (sent to generate replies)
  • Privacy Policy: Please refer to POE's privacy policy

4.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests, such as:

  • Court orders or subpoenas
  • Government investigations
  • Protection of our rights or the rights of others

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Access Your Data

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you. You can view your data through:

  • The App's profile section
  • Contacting us directly (see Contact section)

5.2 Correct Your Data

You can correct inaccurate information by:

  • Updating your profile in the App
  • Contacting us to request corrections

5.3 Delete Your Data

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data:

In-App Deletion:

  1. Open the App
  2. Go to Profile Menu
  3. Select "Manage Account" or "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

What Gets Deleted:

  • Your account information
  • All your messages
  • Your profile data
  • Your subscription information

Note: Some information may be retained in anonymized form for legal or business purposes (e.g., aggregated usage statistics).

5.4 Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw your consent to data collection by:

  • Deleting your account (as described above)
  • Uninstalling the App

5.5 Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data in a portable format by contacting us (see Contact section).

6. About Anonymity and Privacy

6.1 Authentication Requirement

Chatteroo requires a Google email address for authentication. This is necessary because:

  • It ensures secure account access
  • It prevents abuse and spam
  • It enables account recovery
  • It allows us to provide personalized experiences

6.2 Message Privacy

Your messages are private:

  • Messages are only visible to you
  • Other users cannot see your messages
  • Messages are not shared publicly
  • Messages are used solely to generate AI replies

6.3 Email Privacy

Your email is private:

  • Your email is not displayed to other users
  • Your email is not shared with third parties (except service providers)
  • Your email is used only for account management and authentication

6.4 What We Mean by "Privacy-Focused"

While we require email authentication, we are "privacy-focused" in that:

  • We minimize data collection (only what's necessary)
  • We don't sell your data
  • We don't share your data with advertisers
  • We use secure encryption
  • We allow you to delete your data at any time
  • Your conversations remain private and confidential

7. Children's Privacy

Chatteroo is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly.

8. International Users

If you are using Chatteroo from outside the United States, please note that:

  • Your information may be transferred to and stored in servers located in the United States or other countries
  • Data protection laws in your country may differ from those in the United States
  • By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States
